1. Introduction

Welcome to FileHub. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our file sharing service.

FileHub is designed with privacy in mind. Unlike many online services, we do not collect unnecessary data, use tracking technologies, or monetize your information. We believe in providing a straightforward, privacy-respecting service.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. Here's exactly what information we collect and why:

Account Information

  • Username: To identify your account and personalize your experience
  • Email address: For account verification, password resets, and essential service communications
  • Password: Stored securely using industry-standard hashing (we never store plain-text passwords)

Files and Content

  • Uploaded files: The files you choose to upload to our service
  • File metadata: File names, sizes, upload dates, and descriptions you provide
  • Section information: Names and codes for the sections you create to organize your files

IP Address (Rate Limiting Only)

We temporarily collect your IP address exclusively for rate limiting purposes to prevent abuse and ensure fair usage of our service. This is the only technical data we collect about your connection.

Important: We do NOT use your IP address for tracking, analytics, or any other purpose beyond security and rate limiting.

What We DON'T Collect

  • Browsing history or behavior
  • Location data beyond general IP-based region
  • Device information or browser fingerprints
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Analytics or usage patterns
  • Social media information
  • Third-party data from data brokers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve our service:

Service Provision

To create and manage your account, store your files, and enable file sharing functionality

Security & Abuse Prevention

To protect our service from unauthorized access, spam, and malicious activities using rate limiting

Essential Communications

To send account-related emails like password resets and important service updates (no marketing)

Service Maintenance

To maintain and improve the reliability and performance of FileHub

4. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard practices:

Encrypted Storage

All passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage. Files are stored securely on our servers.

Access Controls

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access server infrastructure.

HTTPS/SSL

All data transmission between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS.

Regular Monitoring

We monitor our systems for security threats and unauthorized access attempts.

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data using industry best practices.

5. Data Sharing

We Do NOT Sell or Share Your Data

FileHub does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is yours.

The only circumstances under which we might share information are:

Legal Requirements

If required by law, court order, or government regulation, we may disclose information. We will notify you of such requests unless legally prohibited.

Service Providers

We may use trusted third-party service providers (like hosting services) who need access to your data to operate our service. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.

With Your Consent

We will share information with third parties only when you explicitly authorize us to do so.

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data. Here are your rights:

Access

View and download all your data at any time through your dashboard

Update

Modify your account information and settings whenever you want

Delete

Delete individual files, sections, or your entire account permanently

Export

Download all your files and data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions, please contact us at privacy@filehub.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our service:

Active Accounts: We retain your account information and files as long as your account remains active.
Deleted Files: When you delete files, they are permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
Closed Accounts: When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days, except where we're legally required to retain certain information.
IP Addresses: IP addresses used for rate limiting are retained temporarily and automatically purged after 24 hours.

8. Children's Privacy

FileHub is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@filehub.com.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we will notify you via email or through a prominent notice on our website
  • We encourage you to review this policy periodically

Your continued use of FileHub after changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
