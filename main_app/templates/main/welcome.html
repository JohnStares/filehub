What is FileHub?

FileHub is a free file submission platform built specifically for Nigerian universities. Lecturers and course representatives can create submission links, and students can easily upload their assignments, projects, and coursework.

For Lecturers & Course Reps

Create a submission section with a unique link in seconds. Share it with your class via WhatsApp, Telegram, or email. Students submit their work, and you can view, manage, and download all submissions from one dashboard.

  • Generate unique submission links
  • View all submitted files in real-time
  • Download individual or all files
  • Delete unwanted submissions

For Students

No more hassle with email attachments or confusing submission processes. Simply click the link provided by your lecturer, upload your assignment, and you're done. It's completely free and works on any device.

  • No registration required to submit
  • Upload documents, presentations, PDFs
  • Works on mobile and desktop
  • Get instant submission confirmation

Real-Time Analytics & Management

Track submissions as they come in with live analytics. See who has submitted, download files for presentations or grading, and manage everything from an intuitive dashboard. Perfect for project presentations and coursework management.

  • Live submission tracking
  • Secure file storage
  • Bulk download all submissions
  • Easy file preview and management
